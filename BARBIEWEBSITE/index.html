The Beginning

On March 9, 1959, Ruth Handler(an American Business women) and the famous toy company called Mattel, Inc. had released a new doll which was named Barbie. On the first year of the launch, 300,000 Barbie's were sold. Soon after her success, she would gain new doll family, friends, and eventually had 42 movies animated about her journey through Malibu, with the most recent one being a live action film starring Margot Robbie and Ryan Gossling.
